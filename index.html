Profile

Spider-man

Spider-man, also known as Peter Parker, is a Marvel superhero that got his powers from a spiderbite. He is really fast and very strong. He can climb on walls and also has a sixth sense. He can feel it when danger is near. Spider-man also heals faster than any other human being.
